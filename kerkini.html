Lake Kerkini

Experience Lake Kerkini's nature paradise on a full-day trip. See rare birds and majestic buffalos. Take a boat ride, join a jeep safari, and support conservation efforts. Learn from knowledgeable guides. Enjoy activities at the Travel Ecological Center Kerkini-Belles. Discover the beauty of Kerkini's wetland.

    Itinerary

  • Pick up from the hotel at 06:50 am
  • Drive through Thessaloniki to Serres for a coffee break. Proceed to Lake Kerkini and enjoy welcome drinks
  • Take a boat trip on the lake, followed by an exciting 4x4 drive and enjoy a delicious lunch

    Details

  • Professional guide is included
  • The guided excursion typically lasts around 7 hours
