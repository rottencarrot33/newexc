Vergina & Veria

On this excursion, you'll explore Vergina, the first capital of Ancient Macedonia, and Veria, a city filled with religious history. In Vergina, visit the Royal Tombs Museum and admire the tomb of Philip II, father of Alexander the Great, which houses golden treasures dating back to the 4th century BC. You will learn about the architecture, culture, and daily life of the Macedon Kingdom. Finally, in Veria, explore the breathtaking churches and savor the sweet local treat, revani.
