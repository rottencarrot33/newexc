Mt Athos Cruise

Take a cruise to explore the remarkable Monasteries of Mount Athos. Discover this unique autonomous state, home to ancient monasteries that have thrived for over 1,000 years. Marvel at the impressive Byzantine Monasteries, considered Europe's most distinctive Monks' republic. Visit the charming village of Ouranopolis. Experience the sacredness and exclusivity of this destination and delve into its rich spiritual heritage.
