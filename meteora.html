Meteora

Meteora, Greece's largest archaeological site, is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and official holy place. It features enormous rock formations with Christian Orthodox monasteries on top. The area gained fame as a filming location, including the James Bond movie "For Your Eyes Only." Kalambaka city lies below the monastic rock monoliths and is renowned for its delicious Greek cuisine. Don't miss the chance to explore the centuries-old cliffside monasteries and experience this unique wonder.
