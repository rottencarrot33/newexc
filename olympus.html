Mt Olympus & Dion

Visit Dion, the religious center of the Ancient Macedonians dedicated to the worship of Olympian Zeus. This site, located at the footsteps of the famous Mount Olympus, the mythical home of the Olympus Gods. You will visit the archaeological site and the museum in Dion. After that, you will continue to the traditional village of Litochoro, where you will have free time for a tour and a walk in the valley of Enipeas, on the low slopes of the mountain.
