Afitos By Night

Explore its picturesque alleys and traditional buildings of Afitos, made of unique porolithos stone. Enjoy delicious food in tavernas and experience the vibrant nightlife. Shop at charming tourist stores and savor the stunning views of the Aegean Sea. Immerse yourself in the local culture and join the locals in dancing and singing during summer nights. Afitos awaits you with its charm and excitement!

    Itinerary

  • Pick up from the hotel at 19:45 pm
  • Drive to Afitos village. Upon arrival, enjoy an orientation tour led by knowledgeable local escorts
  • After the tour, you have free time to shop for souvenirs, enjoy cocktails, or have a delicious dinner

    Details

  • Professional guide is included
  • The guided excursion typically lasts around 3 hours
