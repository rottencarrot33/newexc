Sithonia Cruise

Experience the ultimate romantic sunset on a boat in Sithonia, Halkidiki. Sail on the calm Aegean Sea, watch the sun set on the horizon, and indulge in champagne, fruits, and snacks. Embark on an unforgettable day at sea, admiring the sunset off the coast of Diaporos Island. Enjoy a warm welcome with bubbles and create lasting memories on this enchanting sailing adventure.

    Itinerary

  • Pick up from the hotel at 17:30 pm
  • Start your cruise to Diaporos Island
  • Continue your cruise to Singitikos Gulf. Admire sunset and enjoy tasty finger foods

    Details

  • Welcome drink, Fruit Salad and Snacks
  • Boat ticket is included
  • The guided excursion typically lasts around 3 hours
